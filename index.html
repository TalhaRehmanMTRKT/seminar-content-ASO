Introduction

This project explores the core principles behind optimal operation in modern power systems. We focus on strategies to achieve cost-effective, reliable, and sustainable power generation and distribution.

Microgrids

Microgrids are self-contained energy systems that operate independently or with the main grid. They include renewables, storage, and smart loads.
